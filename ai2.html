Various Activites


Speak Code is good enough for almost everything you want to know about coding, whether basics or professionals. We have our own personal teachers for each difficulties.
Mainly the difficulties are — beginner, moderate, hard, professionals.
Each difficulties have a different set of activities that can be taught to them in order to make them a professional coder!

Details


We often host workshops for our students in order to make them understand certain things that they might not be familiar of and get them to know about all of it. We also make them go to other schools and have a meetup or a competition in coding. Hackathons too are held in our club and awards are given to the respective winners!
